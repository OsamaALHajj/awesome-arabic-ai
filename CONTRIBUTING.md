## 🤝 Code of Conduct

By participating, you agree to maintain a respectful, inclusive, and constructive environment. Harassment, discrimination, or disrespectful behavior of any kind will not be tolerated.

---

## ✅ What We're Looking For

We welcome contributions that fit **at least one** of these criteria:

- 🤖 **Arabic-specific AI models** (LLMs, vision, speech, multimodal).
- 📊 **Datasets** for training or evaluating Arabic AI systems.
- 🏆 **Benchmarks and leaderboards** measuring Arabic AI performance.
- 🛠️ **Tools, libraries, or frameworks** that process Arabic text/speech/images.
- 📚 **Academic papers** or surveys on Arabic NLP/AI.
- 🎓 **Courses, tutorials, or educational content** about Arabic AI.
- 🏢 **Companies, startups, or research labs** actively working on Arabic AI.
- 🌐 **Communities, conferences, or workshops** focused on Arabic AI/NLP.
- 📰 **High-quality blogs or newsletters** covering Arabic AI developments.

---

## ❌ What We Won't Accept

To maintain quality, we **will not** accept:

- ❌ Resources unrelated to Arabic AI or NLP.
- ❌ Inactive, deprecated, or abandoned projects (last update > 2 years).
- ❌ Paid courses or commercial products without clear value distinction.
- ❌ Affiliate links or self-promotion without merit.
- ❌ Duplicate entries already listed.
- ❌ Resources with broken or dead links.
- ❌ Low-quality content (poor documentation, no public access).
- ❌ Closed-source tools without clear capabilities or use cases.

## 📝 How to Add a Resource

### Step 1: Fork the Repository

Click the **Fork** button at the top-right of the repository page.

### Step 2: Edit the README

Find the most appropriate section for your resource and add it in **alphabetical order** (unless the section uses a different logical order).

### Step 3: Follow the Format

Each entry **must** follow this exact format:

```markdown
- **[Resource Name](URL)** — Brief one-line description (max ~25 words) explaining what it is and why it's useful.
```

**Examples of good entries:**

```markdown
- **[Jais](https://huggingface.co/inceptionai)** — Family of bilingual Arabic-English LLMs (590M to 70B parameters) by Inception (G42). Apache 2.0.

- **[CAMeL Tools](https://github.com/CAMeL-Lab/camel_tools)** — Comprehensive Python toolkit for Arabic NLP by Columbia University.
```

### Step 4: Submit a Pull Request

- Use a clear PR title: `Add [Resource Name] to [Section]`
- In the description, briefly explain why this resource adds value.
- Reference any related issues if applicable.

---

## 🎨 Formatting Rules

1. **Bold the name** using `**[Name](URL)**`.
2. Use an **em-dash** (`—`) to separate name from description (not a hyphen `-`).
3. Keep descriptions **concise** — one sentence, ideally under 25 words.
4. End descriptions with a **period**.
5. Include **license info** for models/datasets when known (e.g., "Apache 2.0", "MIT", "CC-BY-SA").
6. Use **proper capitalization** for project names (e.g., "HuggingFace", not "huggingface").
7. **Do not** include emojis inside entries (emojis are reserved for section headers).
8. **Do not** include version numbers unless critically relevant.

---

## 🔄 Pull Request Process

1. ✅ Ensure your entry follows the formatting rules above.
2. ✅ Make sure the link works and points to the official source.
3. ✅ Verify the resource is not already listed (use `Ctrl+F` to search).
4. ✅ Place the entry in the correct section and order.
5. ✅ Keep your PR focused — **one resource per PR** is preferred.
6. ✅ Be patient — reviews typically happen within 3–7 days.

**PR Checklist** (copy-paste into your PR description):

```
- [ ] Resource is related to Arabic AI/NLP
- [ ] Entry follows the formatting style
- [ ] Link is working and points to the official source
- [ ] Resource is not a duplicate
- [ ] Placed in the correct section
- [ ] Description is concise and accurate
```

## 🐛 Reporting Issues

Found a problem? Please [open an issue](https://github.com/OsamaALHajj/awesome-arabic-ai/issues/new) with:

- 🔗 **Broken links** — Include the section and entry name.
- 📅 **Outdated info** — Specify what changed.
- 💡 **New category suggestion** — Explain why it deserves its own section.
- 🤔 **General questions** — We're happy to help!
